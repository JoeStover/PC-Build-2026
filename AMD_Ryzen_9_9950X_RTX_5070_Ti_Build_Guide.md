## Introduction
This guide provides a comprehensive walkthrough for assembling a high-performance gaming PC featuring AMD Ryzen 9 9950X and ASUS ROG STRIX RTX 5070 Ti.

## Pre-Build Preparation
1. **Gather Tools and Components:** Make sure you have all required tools (screwdrivers, anti-static wrist strap) and components ready.
2. **Workspace Setup:** Choose a clean, static-free work environment.
3. **Read Manuals:** Familiarize yourself with the manuals of all components.

## Component Overview
- **CPU:** AMD Ryzen 9 9950X
- **Motherboard:** ASUS ROG CROSSHAIR X870E HERO
- **RAM:** G.Skill Trident Z5 RGB 64GB DDR5-6000
- **Storage:** Samsung 990 Pro 2TB
- **GPU:** ASUS ROG STRIX RTX 5070 Ti
- **Case:** Fractal Design Meshify 2 XL
- **Power Supply:** Corsair RM1000x 2024
- **CPU Cooler:** Noctua NH-U12A chromax.black
- **Fans:** 7x Noctua NF-A14x25 G2 PWM fans

## Building the PC
1. **Install CPU:** Place the CPU in the motherboard socket and secure it.
2. **Install RAM:** Insert RAM sticks into the appropriate slots.
3. **Install CPU Cooler:** Attach the cooler on top of the CPU.
4. **Mount Motherboard:** Secure the motherboard inside the case.
5. **Install Storage:** Connect the Samsung 990 Pro to the motherboard.
6. **Install GPU:** Insert the RTX 5070 Ti into the PCIe slot.
7. **Connect Power Supply:** Route power connections from the RM1000x.

## Cable Management
- Plan cable routes before securing them.
- Use cable ties to bundle and organize cables behind the motherboard tray.
- Ensure airflow is unobstructed by the cables.

## Fan Configuration
- Install fans in a way that ensures optimal airflow; typically, front intake and rear exhaust configuration.
- Use PWM control to optimize fan speeds based on temperature.

## BIOS Setup
1. **Enter BIOS:** Press the BIOS entry key during boot.
2. **Load Optimized Defaults:** Reset settings to default for stability.
3. **Configure RAM:** Enable XMP to take advantage of RAM's rated speeds.
4. **Set Boot Order:** Prioritize the storage drive with the OS.
5. **Save and Exit:** Apply settings and reboot.

## Stress Testing
- Use Prime95 and FurMark to stress test the CPU and GPU, ensuring stable performance under load.
- Monitor temperatures to ensure cooling is sufficient.

## Troubleshooting
- If the system does not boot, check power connections and seating of components.
- Utilize forums and resources for specific error codes or issues.
